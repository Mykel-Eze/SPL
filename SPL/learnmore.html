what we are

Trading Business

Samon Petroleum Limited “SPL” is a downstream company that strategic trades gasoil, gasoline, dpk/jet A1 and in the nearest future hopes to also trade bitumen and LPG products. SPL is poised to achieve its place amongst the leading indigenous downstream companies.

Storage Facilities

Samon Petroleum Limited “SPL” solely owns a storage facility in the Southern Region of Nigeria- Calabar Free Trade Zone, Cross River State. The facilities have 4 steel vertical storage tanks are designed to the API 650 and has a capacity of approximately 50 million litres.

  • Product Stored
  • Premium Motor Spirit (PMS)
  • Automotive Gas Oil (AGO)
  • Dual Purpose Kerosene (DPK)

Samon Jetty Facilities

SPL Jetty has the capacity of berthing and discharging up to 20,000-25,000 DWT vessels. We offer throughput logistic and blending of businesses of various products such as LPG, Bitumen and clean petroleum products through an extensive pipeline networks to reach over 35 depots within and outside the Free Trade Zone, Calabar.

SPL Jetty will operate 24/7 and it is equipped with a world-class security system that enables monitoring of the jetty round the clock. With its state-of-the-art security and features such as fire monitors, firefighting pumps, etc.

Our jetty is strategically located at Free Trade Zone, with a wide road that allows easy access to various depots. The distance between the jetty and our depot is about 1.9 KM, which puts us at a great advantage, increases the utilization of existing storage and allows proper time management.

Retail Outlets

Over the years Samon Petroleum has been supplying quality fuel products to end-users, that gets people where they need to go. We are glad to meet our customers’ expectations, played a role in some region in the Eastern part of Nigeria and hope to continue to the journey.

Petroleum Product Depot

Samon Petroleum Limited depot is strategically located within the Free Trade Zone in Calabar, there are seventeen depots within the zone and nine outside the zone.

SPL loading arm operates using the Accuload III.net system, which can dispense 1,500 - 2,000 Litres per Minute on an average. Each product has its dedicated line that is linked from the storage tanks to the loading dock and SPL’s Gantry accommodates 20-25 trucks.
