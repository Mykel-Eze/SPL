Ifesinachi Okeke

MANAGING DIRECTOR/ CEO

Ifesinachi has his career as a wealth manager for Barclays Bank Plc. after which he returned to his roots and joined Arcadia petroleum and has since had tremendous exposure in oil and gas.

During his time at Arcadia, he gained extensive experience in the downstream sector being involved in tanker operations and chartering as well as vessel sales, specifically VLCC and AFRIMAX tankers.
After a couple of years, he assumed a front office role trading clean petroleum products and supervising the trade of fuel oil from West Africa to East and West coast of the United States of America. In this capacity, he played a crucial role in the growth and development of the West African Arm of the business.

Ifesinachi has a Bachelors degree from the University of Nigeria Nsukka and a Masters degree in Hydrocarbon Enterprise from the University of Aberdeen. He also attended the Centre of petroleum studies, Oxford.

Ogonna Okeke

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Ogonna started his career in Fineshade Energy (UK) Limited. after which he returned to Nigeria and joined the Downstream sector, he worked at Bennok Oil Limited as the MD/CEO and he gained a lot of experience in the retail and logistics side of the downstream sector.

He was responsible for the full Retail chain from buying of products at various Depots to the final discharge point at the various retail stations which the company owned and also the logistics arm of the business which handled the various trucks of the company.

He oversees the sales and procurement of all products in Samon petroleum and also the Operations of the entire facility.

Ogonna has a Bachelor’s degree from Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka. He also went to the University of Sheffield in the United kingdom.

Joy Ogbanga

CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER

Joy Ogbanga has over twenty years working experience in direct operational roles relating to vessels, storage facilities, shipping and operations.

In his capacity as the Chief Operating Officer of Samon Petroleum Limited, he is responsible for ensuring the proper execution of all operations cutting across the management of the storage facility, product discharge into tank, proper care and maintenance of products in tank, maintenance of equipment and facilities at the facility, and proper discharge of products to trucks.

Joy has worked extensively across the largest names in the Nigerian downstream business. Prior to Samon Petroleum, he was the Chief Operating Officer at Knights Bridge Energy Ltd, and prior to this, he worked at MRS Oil and Gas Limited as a Manager of Shipping and Operations. In addition, he has previously worked with Sahara Energy Resource Limited as the Head of Operations and Shipping Logistics – a role he did for over ten years. 

Chuka Obi

CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER

Chuka Obi is the Chief Financial Officer of Samon Petroleum Limited. In this role, he oversees all financing activities for the company. 

Prior to his role at Samon, he worked at Argentil Capital Partners as an investment banker advising on over $7bn dollars in executed transactions in the energy space. In this capacity, he provided guidance across the energy value chain with a focus on the African region. 

Chuka's experience also includes roles at Access Bank in Nigeria and Citigroup in New York. 

Nduka Momah

HEAD OF PROJECTS

Nduka Momah has extensive project management experience in managing and driving large roll out and capacity expansion projects. Most of these complex project deliveries have span both locally and in the Sub-Saharan region. He joined Samon Petroleum Limited in 2015 after over 10 years’ experience working for companies like Ericsson and MTN amongst others.

Nduka is an Electronics Engineering graduate from the University of Nigeria Nsukka as well as an associate degree in project management from the George Washington University and Six Sigma certifications.

Nduka oversees the planning and implementation of all Samon Petroleum investment projects relevant to expansions to other components of the supply chain. 

Engr. Kazeem Musa

HEAD OF DEPOT OPERATIONS/DEPUTY DEPOT MANAGER

Engr. Kazeem Musa has been working with Samon since 2014 as the head of operations. Prior to his role at Samon, he worked as a mechanical resident engineer at A.S. Collins & Associates (An Oil & Gas Consultancy Firm) for seven years where worked with storage facilities from operations, supervision and rehabilitation.
